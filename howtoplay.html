How to play Paintful

Pain(t)ful is a simple multiplayer drawing game and most parts of it should be self explanatory.

1. Enter a game

You can either start a new game or join an existing one.
In order to start a new game, press the 'New Game' button and enter your name.
If someone else already created a game, join their game by entering the game ID (anyone that already joined a game can tell you the ID) and clicking on the 'Join Game' button.

2. Start the game

As soon as at least three people joined a game, anyone of them can start it by clicking on the 'Start Game' button.
Now, every player receives an individual quest, i.e. some term consisting of two words which they need to draw. As soon as everyone uploaded their paintings the actual game can start.

3. Guess

The first player's painting is now presented to all other players (while the first player needs to wait). They have to guess what this painting shows and enter the term consisting of two words and submit their guess by clicking on the 'Submit' button.

4. Choose answer

After every player has submitted their guess, all those guesses together with the correct answer are presented to the players. They must decide for one answer by clicking on the respective button.

5. End of round

If every player has made their choice, the score of the first round is presented. The score is calculated as follows:

  • If a player guessed what the artist has drawn, the player and the artist gain 5 points
  • If a player chose the correct answer, the player and the artist gain 2 points
  • If a player chose the wrong answer, the player that submitted the (wrong) guess gains 1 point

6. End of game

Steps 3-5 are now repeated until every painting has been discussed.
Winner is the player with the highest score.
